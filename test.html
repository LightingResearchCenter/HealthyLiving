Room Description

A common area in a senior care facility is where residents can spend most of their day. This 1550 sf common area is divided into two areas for dining and activities. The dining area has round tables with chairs and the activity area has sofas, side tables, and rectangular tables with chairs for recreational use. From eating meals in the dining area, relaxing on the couch, or sitting at the activity tables, designs should aim to reach desired CS levels in these locations.

The lighting design consists of twenty-four 2x4 “center basket” troffers that are spaced 8’x8’ on center within the space.
