Our Role

This website was conceived to bridge recent developments in lighting research and education to lighting applications in real-world situations by providing design suggestions for specific places that meet the unique needs of the people who occupy them. We present it as open-access resource for lighting designers and manufacturers, facilities resource management professionals, and anyone else who is interested in exploring lighting solutions for healthier building and their occupants.
